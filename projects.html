Graphic Design Projects

Some projects made in After Effects and Premiere. All audio was edited in Audition/Audacity, and mastered using Dolby.

Knock 2 - feel u luv me // NEOKRONO REMIX
11/16/24
Dual slider control (One with script, one master control for intensity) allowed for complete control over properties. Despite my attempts to master this track, the balance between the lows and midrange were so good I personally felt like the original version sounded the best. More optimizations were done, improving rendering time to near O(nlogn) time.
PHANTASZZ - PROBLEMS // BASS BOOSTED
10/16/24
Using a single slider control for amplitude control and parenting each property to it allowed for significantly improved render times. Motion is still a bit rough, but it is a showcase of how optimized render times could be.
