About Me

Hello, if you read this far then congrats! I'm a at uOttawa usually researching and developing prototypes related to ESP32/RP2040 embedded systems and electronics hardware design. Aside from that, I also do things such as CAD design, digital systems, and low-level programming (C, C++, Java, Micropython, etc. as well as some React).

Recognized for combining strong technical expertise with commitment, clear communication, and high standards to consistently deliver achieve high-quality outcomes reliable results and keep teams on track.

I'm also working on personal projects such as implementing Reprap in modern 3D printers and low power ICs. Check them out below!

Additionally, I offer 3D printing services as well as specialized repair for Computers, Laptops, Mobile Devices and Bikes. Please contact me for more information on rates.
