CoreXY + Direct Drive

The gantry system uses a rigid frame made completely from salvaged extrusions. This allows for minimal flex and vibrations during high-speed movements.

E3D's V6 Volcano Hotend paired with the Titan Extruder allows for extremely high flowrates, and thus print speeds.

To reduce effects of VFA and ringing at higher speeds, the kinematics involve a hybrid linear rod/rail XY system versus the original T-slot extrusion design.
